## 1. Executive Summary

BackPocket OS is an AI-powered business operating system for small Australian construction and trade businesses ("tradies"). It is transitioning from a general AI assistant to a **mission-critical Digital Twin** for trade businesses (the "One-Man Army" vision). It replaces the chaos of phone calls, paper quotes, and missed Gmail threads with a single intelligent platform that reads emails, extracts leads, generates quotes, sends invoices, tracks payments, and talks back — all via voice or a mobile app.

The target user is a sole-trader or 2–5 person tradie operation making $300k–$2M AUD/year who has zero time for admin and zero tolerance for enterprise software complexity.

**Core value proposition:** "You do the job. BackPocket does the paperwork. Your Digital Twin handles the rest."

---

## 2. Problem Statement

### Who has this problem
Australian tradies (plumbers, electricians, builders, roofers, tilers, concreters) running small businesses:
- **Lead leakage:** Quotes never sent because the tradie was on a job site.
- **Offline Gaps:** Loss of connectivity in sheds or rural areas means tools stop working.
- **Amnesia:** AI forgetting specific pricing or tone preferences after a single session.
- **Trust & Liability:** Fear of AI taking incorrect actions (sending wrong quotes) without oversight.

---

## 4. Product Vision

BackPocket OS is the Digital Twin a sole trader never had. It:
1. **Listens** — reads Gmail, WhatsApp, voice notes (with offline resilience).
2. **Thinks** — extracts leads, validates compliance, calculates quotes via specialized Estimator and Site Manager personas.
3. **Acts** — sends follow-ups, issues invoices, chases payments (with Human-in-the-loop verification).
4. **Learns** — adapts to each tradie's pricing, tone, and job patterns, promoting "learned" preferences to permanent rules.

## 5. System Architecture

### 5.4 AI Twin System

Three specialized AI personas (Digital Twins) run in parallel:

| Twin | Role | Color | Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Estimator Twin** | Measurements, parsing construction leads, calculating quotes/payments. | Blue | Quote creation, lead ingestion |
| **Site Manager Twin** | Documents, OCR, marketing content, site visit notes. | Green | Document upload, social posts |
| **Admin Twin** | Inbox triage, scheduling, workflow orchestration. | Red | Email triage, voice commands |

## 6. Feature Requirements

### 6.1 Email Triage & Human-in-the-loop
- FR-E1: Connect Gmail via OAuth 2.0.
- FR-E10: **Human-in-the-loop**: Every AI-generated draft or action must have a "Verify & Send" button. AI never takes destructive action without explicit user confirmation.

### 6.5 Voice Command Interface & Offline Resilience
- FR-V1: Flutter FAB microphone button.
- FR-V7: **Offline Voice Resilience**: If no network (4G/Wi-Fi) is available, the mobile app queues transcripts/audio blobs in local storage (`sqflite`). Pip processes them automatically once reception returns.

### 6.8 Agentic RAG & Permanent Memory
- FR-R1: Every AI-generated action stored in vector DB.
- FR-R7: **Permanent Memory**: Real-time corrections or feedback given in TwinChat can be promoted to permanent rules. Pip asks: "Should I remember this rule for next time?". If confirmed, it is saved to the `instructions` table.
