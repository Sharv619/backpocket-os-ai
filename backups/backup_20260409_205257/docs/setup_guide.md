# 🗺️ BackPocket Twin: Setting Up Your Smart Robot (Guide) 🚀

This is the easy guide to connect your other businesses to the **BackPocket Twin**. 

---

## 👂 Part 1: How to make the Twin "Hear" (Forwarding)
*The goal: Send mail from your other brands to your main hub: `cherry@bigbossgroup.com.au`.*

### 🟢 For Google Accounts (e.g., `yourwebaccountant.com`)
1.  Log in to the brand you want to forward **FROM**.
2.  Click the **Gear icon** (Settings).
3.  Click **Forwarding and POP/IMAP**.
4.  Click **Add a forwarding address** and type `cherry@bigbossgroup.com.au`.
5.  **Check your main hub email** for a "Secret Code" and type it in.
6.  Click **Save Changes** at the bottom.

---

## 🗣️ Part 2: How to make the Twin "Talk" (The Hats)
*The goal: Let the Twin reply using your brand name (like "Your Web Accountant").*

1.  Log in to your **Main Hub** (`cherry@bigbossgroup.com.au`).
2.  Go to **Settings** > **Accounts and Import**.
3.  Find **"Send mail as"** and click **Add another email address**.
4.  Type your name and the brand email.
5.  **The Secret Setting**: Scroll down to "When replying to a message" and click **"Reply from the same address the message was sent to."**

---

## 🌐 Part 3: The Secret Tunnel (Ngrok)
*The goal: Connect your computer to your phone.*

1.  Open the black terminal box on your computer.
2.  Type: `.\ngrok.exe http 8000`.
3.  A web address will appear (starts with `https://`).
4.  Copy that address and paste it into your **Whapi.cloud Settings**.

---

## 🏆 Part 4: The Final Test!
1.  Send a test email to your brand.
2.  Wait 2 minutes.
3.  **Check your WhatsApp!** If you see a message, the Twin is alive! 🤖✨
