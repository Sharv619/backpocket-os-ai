## 2. Pricing — The Hybrid Approach

### Structure

| Tier | Price | What It Is | What Steve Gets |
|------|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **The Engine** | **$299 one-off** | Local-first install, System of Record, lifetime license to the base platform | Ownership. His leads, quotes, notes, photos, client history — all stored locally, his forever. Never a hostage to our billing. |
| **The Fuel** | **$15 / month** | AI processing credits (Pip) — drafts, voice transcription, photo analysis, social posts | Ongoing intelligence. Every week Pip gets smarter as models get cheaper. |

### Why This Structure Wins

1.  **High upfront cash** → we're profitable per-customer from Day 1. No 18-month CAC payback death march.
2.  **Psychological ownership** → $299 says "you own this", not "you rent software". Tradies hate rent.
3.  **Sustainable partnership** → $15/mo is below the "cancel-on-a-bad-day" threshold. Less than a slab.
4.  **Margin expansion built-in** → AI inference costs drop ~40% YoY; our price stays flat; fuel margin compounds.
5.  **Local-first defensibility** → if Pip goes offline, The Engine still works. Refund risk is bounded.

## 3. Go-to-Market — Western Sydney First

### Why Western Sydney

-   **Density**: 700k+ SMB tradies in the Parramatta / Blacktown / Penrith / Campbelltown corridor.
-   **Pain concentration**: long commutes from home base to sites → quoting-while-driving is a *killer* wedge.
-   **Word-of-mouth velocity**: tradies refer each other within suburb-scoped WhatsApp/Facebook groups. Land 10, win 100.
-   **We're local**: geographic proximity to pilot customers = 48hr iteration loop.

### Beachhead Segment

**"Steve"** — 28–55 yo solo or 2–3 person tradie operator, $200k–$800k annual revenue, in **plumbing, electrical, carpentry, or tiling**. Has a smartphone. Hates admin. Partner usually does the books.

### GTM Phases

#### Phase 1 — Design Partner (Weeks 0–8)
-   **10 Design Partners** (we call them "Pioneers"): free Engine + 12 months Fuel in exchange for weekly feedback calls.
-   Recruit via: Parramatta Business Chamber, two local tradie WhatsApp groups, founder's direct network.
-   **Goal:** 3 case studies with hard numbers ("Mick saved 11 hrs/week, won 2 extra jobs").

#### Phase 2 — Paid Pilot (Weeks 9–16)
-   Launch at **$199 one-off + $15/mo** (founder's pricing) for first 100 customers.
-   Channels:
    -   **Facebook Groups**: "Sydney Tradies Network", "Western Sydney Plumbers", etc.
    -   **TikTok + Instagram Reels**: Steve-POV content ("This is my week BEFORE Pip... this is AFTER").
    -   **Local trade suppliers**: Reece, Tradelink, Bunnings trade counters — leaflet drop + QR code.
    -   **Referral**: $50 credit for every Pioneer who refers a paying customer.
-   **Goal:** 100 paying customers by end of Week 16.

#### Phase 3 — Scale (Weeks 17–52)
-   Raise pricing to **$299 + $15/mo** (the public rate).
-   Open waitlist → drip-launch to build FOMO.
-   Add **industry verticals** (landscapers, mobile mechanics) by cloning the `services/construction.py` pattern.
-   Expand geographically: Melbourne NE suburbs, SE Queensland, Perth.
-   **Goal:** 1,000 paying customers by Month 12.

### Channel Mix (Year 1 Budget Split)

| Channel | % of Spend | Why |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| Founder-led sales + referrals | 40% | Zero CAC, highest trust in this segment |
| Short-form video (TikTok/Reels) | 25% | Tradies scroll in the ute between jobs |
| Facebook Groups (organic + boosted) | 20% | Native to the audience |
| Trade supplier partnerships | 10% | High-intent physical touchpoint |

## 4. The "Why Now" Narrative

### Three Forces Collided in 2025

1.  **GenAI hit voice-first reliability.** Whisper + small on-device LLMs (Llama 3.2, Gemma 2) now run on a $500 phone. Noisy job sites, gloves on, reading glasses in the ute — tradies finally have a keyboard that isn't a keyboard.
2.  **Tradie burnout is a public health story.** Australian Men's Health Forum 2025 data: 41% of solo tradies work 55+ hrs/week; partner-reported relationship strain up 22% since 2020. Admin is the single largest non-billable time sink.
3.  **The regulatory squeeze is here.** New ATO e-invoicing rules (Peppol) and GST reporting automation mean tradies *need* a System of Record — paper carbon books are no longer compliant.

**If we don't win this category in the next 18 months, ServiceM8 adds AI and the window closes.**

## 6. Competitive Moat

1.  **System of Record (not AI wrapper)**: Steve's data lives locally. If we vanish, he keeps working. Competitors are SaaS-only.
2.  **Local-first AI**: Ollama + on-device embeddings in the Flutter app. Our inference cost per user trends toward zero. OpenAI-based competitors' costs scale linearly.
3.  **"Pip" character + voice UX**: branded, warm, voice-first. Tradies won't tolerate chatbot UX; they *will* tolerate an AI mate named Pip.
4.  **Data flywheel**: every correction ("Cherry said X but should have said Y") trains the private RAG. The longer Steve uses us, the better his Twin gets — and that's portable data *he owns*.
5.  **Distribution lock-in**: once we're in 3 suburb-scoped WhatsApp groups, we are the default recommendation. SaaS competitors cannot buy their way past word-of-mouth in this segment.
