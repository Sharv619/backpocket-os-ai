## Slide 5: How It Works
**Your Digital Twin in Action**

1. **Email arrives** → Twin reads it instantly
2. **AI Triage** → Urgent = alert now, Normal = batch later, Spam = auto-archive
3. **AI Drafts** → Writes a response that sounds like you wrote it
4. **You Approve** → One WhatsApp message: "approve 00001"
5. **Sent!** → Email goes out from the right account

*"You stay in control. AI does the heavy lifting."*
