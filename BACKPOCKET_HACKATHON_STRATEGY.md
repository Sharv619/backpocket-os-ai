## 🏗️ The Technical Edge (Our "Moat")
> *"Agentic RAG—AI that doesn't just search, it acts. Custom 'Lifecycle Hooks' bridge the gap between an email and WhatsApp. Local execution means your data stays in your 'back pocket'."*

### Technical Talking Points:
1.  **Agentic RAG**: Unlike simple RAG, we don't just find text; we interpret business logic and decide on the next action (Triage vs. Draft vs. Search).
2.  **Lifecycle Hooks**: Trigger-based architecture. 
    - `OnEmailReceived` -> AI Triage -> WhatsApp Alert.
    - `OnUserApproved` -> Gmail Send.
3.  **Local/Privacy**: Built for solo traders who can't risk their business data in the cloud. We run locally on Mini-PC hardware.
